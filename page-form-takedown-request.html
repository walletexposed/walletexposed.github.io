Submit a Takedown Request


Do you know of false information on the Wallet.Exposed website?
Is there a social media post which you feel needs to be taken down?


You can use our Takedown Request Form below in order to officially request that information be removed from the Wallet.Exposed website and/or services.

Feel free to provide only as many details as you are comfortable sharing.
The more information you provide, the more likely it is that we will be able to validate your story.
You can always follow-up with us in the future; as long as you use the same email account we can verify you as the sender of any previous messages.


All submitted webform data is routed through Formspree into our secure ProtonMail inbox, in accordance with our Privacy Policy.
